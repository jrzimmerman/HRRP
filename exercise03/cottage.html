The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden the uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornimental and edible plants.

The cottage garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estate gardens.

The earlier cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with and emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
